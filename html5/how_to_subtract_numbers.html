How to subtract numbers

Here you will find instructions about using the subtraction features of the JPS Calculator app. This is an exmaple of a high-level DITA topic. Tasks are the majority of the content types used in software documentation.

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what subtraction is.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the minus (-) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.
The result from the subtraction is displayed.
You want to subtract 2 from 3. The equation will look like 3-2=1. You enter 3. Press minus. Enter 2. Press equals.

The result 1 appears on the screen.

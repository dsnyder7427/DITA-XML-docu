What is the subtraction of numbers?

To successfully perform the task of substracting numbers, you must know what subtraction is. The term "subtraction" represents a mathematical operation. Subtraction is a process, an action you perform using numbers. Subtraction allows you to find the difference of two numbers.

Use the minus (-) sign to express the app's subtraction operation.

In the subtraction process, take a number to reduce it to a smaller number. That is, take away one number from another.
